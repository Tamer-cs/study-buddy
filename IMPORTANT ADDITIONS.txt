***Gap Taxonomy and what do they lead to: 
1- Conceptual Confusion → clarify the distinction, use contrast and analogy
2- Procedural Application Gap → walk through the thinking process step by step, out loud
3- Intuition Gap → ask why, force justification, expose the reasoning behind the correct answer
4- Incomplete Understanding → identify exactly where the reasoning stopped and push from there

***why is studybuddy novel: 
Existing AI tutors (Khanmigo, ChatGPT) guide students toward answers but don't diagnose why reasoning fails. They are course-agnostic — they reason from general training data, not from what was actually taught. Study Buddy addresses a different problem: given a student's answer to an exam question, grounded in their specific course material, classify the cognitive gap type and deliver feedback that teaches the thinking process THAT FILLS THIS EXACT GAP, not the answer.
